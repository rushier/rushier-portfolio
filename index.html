Unlocking the Power of Large Models

With the development of ChatGPT, the term large model gradually became known and changed people's lives, such as intelligent question answering, sentiment analysis, medical diagnosis report generation, etc. Over the past few years, the definition of large model has changed constantly. In the early days, models with millions or tens of millions of parameters were considered large models. Nowadays, however, models with hundreds of millions or even billions of parameters are becoming more common due to the increasing number of parameters and computational complexity of models. When the model size is large to a certain extent, the performance will increase dramatically, which is defined as the emergent ability of large models. I am currently working on developing large-scale models for medical applications, specifically in the field of 3D medical image segmentation. This technology is used to automatically segment organs or lesions throughout the entire body.
